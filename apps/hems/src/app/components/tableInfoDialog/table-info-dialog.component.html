Room nr:

The guest's room number.

Name:

The guest's last name.

Time of arrival:

The time of the guest's arrival.

Nr of bags:

The number of bags the guest has. If the luggages sizes vary then write the number of main suitcases, add a “+” sign followed by the number of other items.

Tag nr:

The tag number found at the bottom of the luggage tag.

BB in LR:

(Bellboy in Luggage room) The initials of the bell boy who placed the luggage in the luggage room.

Location:

The location where the luggage has been stored. Can be LR, LR1, S1, Room or other. If others please do a further explanation in the comments.

Time in room:

The time when the luggage was placed in the guest's room.

BB up:

(Bellboy up) The initials of the bell boy who delivered the luggage to the guest's room.

Comments:

If something needs further explanation.
